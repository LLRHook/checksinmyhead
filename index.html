Share Bills, Not Your Data

Checkmate is an open source bill-splitting app that respects your privacy. No accounts, no cloud storage, just a simple way to divide expenses with friends.

Why Choose Checkmate

Features designed with data privacy in mind.

Zero Cloud Storage

Your data never leaves your device. We don't store your information in the cloud, so there's nothing to leak or sell.

No Accounts Required

Use Checkmate without creating an account. No email, no passwords, no personal data collection.

Fair Split Calculations

Split bills proportionally based on what each person ordered, including tax and tip distribution.

Easy Sharing

Share bill breakdowns via text message or any messaging app without storing contact information.

Bill History

Review past bills with local storage only. Your history stays on your device and nowhere else.

Dark Mode Support

Enjoy a beautiful interface that adapts to your system preferences with light and dark themes.

Privacy: A Core Value, Not a Feature

In today's digital landscape, users have grown accustomed to trading their personal data for convenience. With each new account creation, individuals surrender pieces of their digital identity to companies that often treat this information as a commodity.

Checkmate was built on a fundamental belief: you shouldn't need to create an account to split a bill with friends.

How It Works

Split bills in four easy steps

Select Participants

Choose who's splitting the bill - no need to create accounts or access your contacts.

Enter Bill Details

Add items, subtotal, tax, and tip. Customize to match your receipt exactly.

Assign Items

Easily assign items to people with flexible splitting options for shared items.

Share Results

Get a clean breakdown of who owes what and share it directly with your friends.
